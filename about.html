Who We Are

Teampiel was born in 1999 from the desire to create a juncture between the manufaturing of luxury leather and high end brands, with a keen eye to the clientele.

In the Florentine area, Teampiel's most important values are the artisanship of the products and the attention to the client, leading to the creation of innovative products.

Today Teampiel is a reference point in the fashion world to the most important luxury brands, thanks to the research and quality control of materials and the constant attention given to workmanship.

Our products are more than just leather: everything starts from an idea, an insight, which can happen during a brainstorm, or simply driving around the countryside. From there everything begins to take shape. We envision the kind of material to use, the particular processes and colours. We give life to our creations as a painter does to his paintings, just on a different canvas.
